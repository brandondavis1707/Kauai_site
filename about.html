About This Site

This website was created as part of a web design project to share practical, easy-to-scan travel info for island of Kauaʻi. It’s meant to be simple, visual, and helpful for planning. Not overwhelming.

Purpose

What this website is

The goal is to organize key travel topics into a clean layout so a first-time visitor can quickly understand what Kauaʻi is like, what to do, and how to plan smarter (without spending the whole trip stuck on their phone).

Audience

Who it’s for

This site is for travelers who want a quick, high-level guide — especially first-time visitors who need help with geography, driving expectations, planning tips, and a few top activity ideas.

Content

What you’ll find here

  • Things to Do: a short list of popular activities and experiences.
  • Food & Dining: a mix of sit-down spots and casual eats.
  • Maps & Locations: regional breakdowns and planning context.
  • Best Time to Visit: weather/season basics to help you choose dates.
  • Travel Tips: realistic expectations + simple prep advice.
Design

Design goals

  • Keep pages consistent so navigation feels predictable.
  • Use readable spacing and sections instead of giant blocks of text.
  • Use visuals to support content (without clutter).
  • Make it responsive so it works on phones and laptops.

Disclaimer

This site is for educational purposes as part of a course project. It is not an official tourism website. Information is based on public travel resources, general knowledge, and personal experience. Details may change over time.
