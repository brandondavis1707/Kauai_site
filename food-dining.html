Food & Dining

Kauai has food for every vibe and taste bud. From quick bites and shave ice to elegant sit down dinners, these are some top locations to help you plan your meals without overthinking it.

Casual

Duke’s

Lihue - East Side

A reliable first stop when you land and want something easy and familiar.

Food Truck

Scorpacciata Neapolitan Pizza

Kapa'a - East Side

Tucked between buildings in downtown Kapaa, this spot serves some of the best pizza on the island. Chewy crust, fresh ingredients, and a great outdoor vibe at solid prices.

Dessert

Pink's Ice Cream Shop

Hanalei - North Shore

A variety of shakes & smoothies, plus Hawaiian grilled cheese in quaint digs.

Dessert

The Fresh Shave

Koloa - South Shore

Another top shave ice option if you are staying in the south.

Upscale

The Beach House

Poipu - South Shore

Beachfront eatery featuring Pacific Rim cuisine & cocktails, plus striking views during sunset for a memorable dinner.

Casual

Porky’s

Koloa - South Shore

A laid back local favorite for BBQ plates and comfort food. Big portions, bold flavors, and the kind of spot you hit when you want something filling without the fuss.

Food Truck

808 Grill

Kapa'a - East Side

Pacific Rim flavors with French and Asian influence. From poke of the day to wings and calamari, this spot reminds you real fast that you’re on a volcanic island in the middle of the Pacific.

Food Truck

Da Melting Pot

Kapa'a - East Side

Burgers, shrimp plates, soft shell crab, and seared ahi served fast without feeling like fast food. The garlic shrimp plate is a standout and worth the mess.

Upscale

Merriman’s

Poipu - South Shore

An upscale option worth planning ahead for a special evening.

Casual

Kountry Kitchen

Kapa'a - East Side

Perfect for grabbing something sweet during the day.
